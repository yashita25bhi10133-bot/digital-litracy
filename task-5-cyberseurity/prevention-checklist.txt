Stay Safe Online – Checklist
Do not share passwords or OTPs with anyone
Use strong and unique passwords
Avoid clicking on suspicious links
Verify emails before responding
Enable two-factor authentication
Do not share personal or financial details online
Always check official websites for opportunities
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi
UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN
Double-check payment requests before approving

Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
